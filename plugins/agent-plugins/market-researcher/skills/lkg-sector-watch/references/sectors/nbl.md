## Context lines (apply, don't restate daily)

- NBL season runs Sep–Mar; NBA runs Oct–Jun — the offset creates crossover and
  attention windows.

  Ball, Josh Giddey, Alex Sarr; G League Ignite folded 2024).
- Established pattern: NBL teams play NBA pre-season games in the US.
